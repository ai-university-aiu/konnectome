PART FIVE - OTHER CONTEXT YOU SHOULD KNOW (SHARP EDGES AND HONEST NON-CLOSURES)


• THE PACK-NAME SHADOWING HAZARD (discovered by building, will bite again). A konnectome pack may not share a name with ANY module file under PrologAI's packs - the library path gives PrologAI's directories priority, so a clashing konnectome pack silently loads the wrong module. This is why the prediction pack is named prediction_loop (bare "prediction" collided with a module inside PrologAI's zoom pack). Before creating any pack, check the candidate name against every PrologAI module filename.

• THE 4.0.0 FINISHING LIST lives at ~/output/Causalontology_4_0_0_ToDo.txt and in the release plan (causalontology docs/Causalontology_4_0_0_Release_Plan.txt): fourteen bindings still need their folded 3.0.0-plus-4.0.0 delta (blocker: their toolchains are not installed on this machine), registry publication for the five green bindings needs the owner's credentials, and the tag-driven ecosystems (Swift, Zig, Go) must not be published until their code is green under a fresh packaging tag.
