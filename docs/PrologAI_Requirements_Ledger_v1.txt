PROLOGAI REQUIREMENTS LEDGER - THE CONSOLIDATED PROGRAM-WIDE VIEW  (v1)

This is the single, canonical, navigable consolidation of every finding the
Connectome program has produced, gathered from the ten per-repository Ledgers into
one account. It writes no code and discovers no new gap; it synthesizes what the
program has already found. The per-repository Ledgers remain the authoritative
per-repository record; this document cites them and does not alter them.

Author: D. R. Dison / ai-university-aiu.

Acronyms are spelled out in full on first use, with the acronym in parentheses, and
the bare acronym thereafter. External proper names are kept verbatim (SWI-Prolog,
Ed25519, SQLite, Git, Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus). A causal_relation_object
(CRO) is a Causalontology structure record; a reproduction-prediction-error signal is
spelled as it appears in the source. The word "stratum" is a Causalontology level of
organization (macromolecular, cellular, synaptic, region, community-and-society, and
so on), each with an ordinal.

SECTION ONE - WHAT THIS IS AND HOW TO READ IT
================================================================================

PART ONE - THE PROGRAM'S PURPOSE, AND WHY THIS LEDGER IS THE PRODUCT

The Connectome program's stated purpose is to discover, by building a vertical slice
of Nature's Cognitive Architecture, WHAT PROLOGAI STILL LACKS to become a complete
language for building any brain region, interface, neurotransmitter, or hormone. The
method is enforce-then-build: a wall a region or an arm hits becomes a Ledger entry;
a load-bearing entry becomes a PrologAI construct; the next region rests on that
construct rather than re-inventing it. The findings are the product. A construct that
"did not need building" is as much a result as one that did.

Those findings were, until now, scattered - one Ledger per repository, each in its
own identifier series. That was correct while building (each instrument records its
own findings). But the PRODUCT of the program is the union of them, read as one
document: a single account of what PrologAI can and cannot yet express. This is that
account.

PART TWO - STATUS MEANINGS

 - OPEN: recorded, not yet closed. PrologAI cannot yet express the thing; the source
   proposed a minimum remedy that has not been built.
 - PARTIALLY CLOSED: a delivery closed the concrete case the finding cited, but the
   general case remains open (there is exactly one: L5).
 - CLOSED: a PrologAI delivery (a construct, with a commit) closed it, OR - for the
   two Wave 8.5 honesty entries - an honest classification resolved it (the construct
   was correctly declared but, being inert on a deterministic predicate, needed no
   change). Every CLOSED entry names what closed it.

================================================================================
SECTION TWO - THE OPEN GAPS: WHAT PROLOGAI STILL LACKS
================================================================================

This is the forward agenda and the most important section. It lists every finding
whose status is OPEN or PARTIALLY CLOSED, grouped by THEME and DEDUPLICATED BY
UNDERLYING CAUSE: where several entries are the same gap seen in different regions,
they are collapsed into one gap with its sightings listed. The themes are ordered by
how load-bearing and how often-recurring the gap is - the first four are the walls a
region cannot get past without a workaround, and are the substance of Section Six.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME A - TEMPORAL AND SCHEDULING  (highest priority; a hard wall)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The underlying gap. PrologAI has no temporal or scheduled construct: no way to run a
process after a delay, at a tick, or over time. The Lattice is reactive (it wakes on
a WRITE, through await and notify) but has no timer, no wall-clock wakeup, and no
scheduled reactivation. Sharper still, the Causalontology temporal model's unit field
is a wall-clock enumeration with NO ordinal or tick unit, so even REPRESENTING
discrete-tick timing forces a distortion. A timing-critical process can be described
as data but not enacted, and tick-based time cannot be recorded natively at all.

Sightings (collapsed):
 - HIPPO-2 (hippocampus, severity Medium). Systems consolidation is DELAYED - a
   labile trace becomes durable over time - but nothing schedules a deferred
   consolidation, so the region consolidates synchronously, right after the synaptic
   write. The delay lives in the CRO's temporal window
   (minimum_delay:1, maximum_delay:3600, unit:"seconds") but is never enacted.
   Evidence: packs/region_stratum/prolog/region_stratum.pl:300 calls
   macromolecular_stratum_consolidate/2 immediately after the store deposit (:313);
   temporal field at packs/macromolecular_stratum/prolog/macromolecular_stratum.pl:116.
   Minimum remedy: a Lattice timer (an await with a scheduled wakeup that fires
   without a write), or a "deferred reactivation" construct that re-posts a cue after
   a modelled delay.
 - CEREBELLUM-1 (cerebellum, severity High; a sharper second sighting of HIPPO-2).
   Timing is the cerebellum's defining function, and it hit two walls: (1) no
   ordinal/tick temporal unit, so minting the correction CRO with unit:"ticks" is
   REJECTED by the conformance engine and the region is forced to record "seconds" (a
   distortion) while the runtime keeps an honest ordinal tick(N); and (2) nothing
   enacts the timing even when represented. Evidence:
   packs/region_stratum/prolog/region_stratum.pl, region_stratum_timing/2 returns
   tick(Iteration); the correction CRO's temporal field is forced from "ticks" to
   "seconds" to validate. Minimum remedy (two parts): add an ordinal/tick unit to the
   Causalontology temporal model, AND a scheduled/temporal construct to enact a timed
   process.

Why it is first. It is the only theme rated High that a region hit as a genuine wall
on its defining function (the cerebellum could not do timing natively), it recurred
and SHARPENED across two regions, and its remedy is a new language affordance, not a
convention. Anything time-based - decay, spaced repetition, replay, delayed
consolidation, inter-stimulus intervals - is blocked on it.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME B - CROSS-STRATAL SEAMS AND MANAGED SKIPS  (highest priority; most recurring)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The underlying gap. A process or relation legitimately spans NON-ADJACENT strata (a
hormone descends ten levels; consolidation rises from molecule to region; a social
context overrides an action). PrologAI, through Causalontology's skips:true flag,
honestly records the jump - the classifier returns "skipping" with an empty skip-gap
list, so "the absence of a mechanism is a finding, not a gap." But three things it
cannot do: (a) MODEL the intervening mechanism as a chain of adjacent-stratum steps;
(b) DISTINGUISH "a mechanism exists but is deliberately unmodeled here" from "no
mechanism exists" (the honest-ignorance distinction); and (c) give a cross-stratal
construct that belongs to an EDGE between strata a HOME - the pack-per-stratum rule
has no place for it, so it piles into the coarser stratum arbitrarily. This is the
program's most-recurring gap: it returned in six places, wherever a process spanned
non-adjacent strata.

Sightings (collapsed):
 - P1 and P2 (slice, severity High and Medium; the origin). Cortisol's skip crosses
   community-and-society (ordinal 14) to macromolecular (ordinal 4) with no
   intervening mechanism; the skip is legible to the STRUCTURE but invisible to the
   RUNTIME (nothing emits "a skip just happened" as a queryable event). Evidence:
   packs/causal_map/prolog/causal_map.pl:209; the runtime only prints it,
   packs/thalamus/prolog/thalamus.pl:59-64.
 - STRATA-2 (strata arm, severity High). Cross-stratal Causalontology kinds - ports,
   conduits, bridges, and skipping CROs - have NO single-stratum home, so the
   one-pack-per-stratum rule must assign them arbitrarily (here, to the coarsest
   stratum they touch). Evidence: all ports, both conduits, and the bridge minted in
   packs/region_stratum/prolog/region_stratum.pl, each reaching down for a finer
   occurrent. Minimum remedy: a first-class cross-stratal (edge) construct with a
   checkable HOME rule.
 - ARBITER-2 (arbiter, severity Medium; second sighting of P2). The contextual
   override crosses five strata carrying skips:true; the mechanism (the cortical /
   hyperdirect pathway) EXISTS but is deliberately unmodeled by this thin cut, and
   there is still no way to say that as distinct from "no mechanism exists."
   Evidence: packs/community_stratum/prolog/community_stratum.pl.
 - HIPPO-3 (hippocampus, severity Low; recurrence of P1/P2). The consolidation CRO
   jumps macromolecular (4) to region (9), skipping cellular (6) and synaptic (7).
 - CEREBELLUM-2 (cerebellum, severity Low; recurrence). The consolidation CRO,
   macromolecular durable depression (4) to region correction (9), skips:true.
 - AMYGDALA-2 (amygdala, severity Low; recurrence). The re-engaged cortisol skip,
   community-and-society (14) to macromolecular (4), skips:true, no modelled
   hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) pathway.

Minimum remedy (shared). A managed-seam construct that (a) records an intentional
cross-stratal jump as more than a boolean - carrying whether a mechanism exists and
is unmodeled, or genuinely does not exist; (b) can be a chain of adjacent-stratum
steps when the pathway is drawn; and (c) gives a cross-stratal construct a home rule
("a spanning construct belongs to the coarsest stratum it touches") that the language
checks rather than each arm conventionally choosing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME C - THE STRUCTURE-TO-DYNAMICS SEAM (GROUNDING FIT)  (high priority; recurring)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The underlying gap. Every neurochemical and every computing construct is TWO things
at once: a grounded Causalontology STRUCTURE record and a native DYNAMICAL law or
variable. The grounding rule (ground the structure, keep the dynamics native) is
correct and was honoured - but PrologAI has no construct that BINDS the two halves by
identity. They are related only by a shared English word. This is distinct from the
cross-stratal seam (Theme B, which is vertical, across levels); this seam is between a
record and the native code it stands for.

Sightings (collapsed):
 - P1 (slice, severity High; the origin, shared with Theme B). Cortisol's structural
   skip CRO lives in causal_map; its dynamical suppression law lives in
   neurochemistry; nothing links them but the label. There is no first-class notion
   of "a hormone" that HAS a structural record AND a dynamical state AND a modulation
   law. Minimum remedy: a modulator/hormone binding construct carrying the id of its
   skip record, a dynamical state variable, and the target and law of its modulation.
 - P3 (slice, severity High). The three-factor plasticity rule
   (change proportional to rate, presynaptic, postsynaptic, dopamine, and cortisol
   suppression) is correctly not one of the seventeen Causalontology kinds; its
   structural proxy (a computational conduit whose transform is a CRO) is a DISCRETE
   cause-to-effect claim that cannot carry the learning rate, the gating, or the
   scaling. Minimum remedy: a "realized_by" reference from a record's transform to
   the NAMED native law that realizes it.
 - P4 (slice, severity Medium). Dopamine as a reward-prediction-error signal is
   correctly a native signal, but there is no construct for a neurotransmitter as a
   typed SIGNAL on the Lattice carrying value, releasing nucleus, and decay, bound by
   identity to the transmissive-conduit structure record. Minimum remedy: a typed
   signal Lattice relation (value, source_port_id, timestamp).
 - STRATA-5 (strata arm, severity Medium; second sighting of P1). The stratum cut
   CANNOT heal the seam: the native dynamics are - correctly, per the grounding rule -
   not grounded and so have NO stratum, so structure and dynamics can never share a
   stratum pack. The seam is not only unhealed but structurally unavoidable under
   one-pack-per-stratum.

Minimum remedy (shared). One of the per-sighting remedies generalized: a construct
that binds a grounded record to the named native predicate or Lattice signal that
realizes it, so the glass box can trace from "what a synapse computes" (structure) to
"how it computes it" (dynamics) through one identity, not a coincidence of labels.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME D - AFFECT AND APPRAISAL REPRESENTATION  (high priority; new, load-bearing)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The underlying gap. There is no first-class construct for a persisted, modulatory
AFFECTIVE STATE - a valence and salience (or mood) that persists across stimuli and
COLOURS later processing, and whose very legality can depend on that persisted
context. The earlier regions selected, stored, and corrected; none had to HOLD an
affective state. Two concrete walls: (1) the membership contract, even its accessor
form, guards the OUTPUT value alone - its test goal is called with only the committed
value and cannot receive CONTEXT - so to make a valence's legality depend on the
cortisol regime the region had to SMUGGLE the regime into the committed value
(appraisal(Valence, Salience, Regime) rather than appraisal(Valence, Salience)); and
(2) the learned association and the cortisol tone that must persist and modulate are
carried as ordinary Lattice/module data, not as a first-class affect construct.

Sighting:
 - AMYGDALA-1 (amygdala, severity High; a new gap, no parent). Evidence:
   packs/region_stratum/prolog/region_stratum.pl, region_stratum_legal_appraisal/1
   and region_stratum_valence_in_regime/2 - the Regime field exists only so the
   stateless accessor can judge legality in context;
   region_stratum_condition_tick/1 carries the persisted affect (assoc, cortisol) as
   plain state-dict fields. Minimum remedy: a first-class affective-state (or
   modulatory-context) construct, plus a context-aware membership accessor that can
   consult a held context when judging legality.

Why it is here despite a single sighting. It is High severity and load-bearing: the
whole limbic system (affect, motivation, mood, emotional modulation) rests on holding
and propagating an affective state, and PrologAI cannot yet do it as a construct.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME E - THE LAYER CONSTRUCT ACROSS REPOSITORIES AND INSIDE PACKS  (recurring)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Underlying gap E-1 (cross-repository). The strict layer rule (L4, delivered) is
single-repository: its scan builds its owner map from one packs directory, so every
cross-repository import is invisible; its layer INTEGERS are a per-repository
namespace with no global coordinate; its Continuous Integration (CI) entry point is
hard-wired to PrologAI's own packs, so a downstream repository must write its own
wrapper; and the rule is declared for only a few packs.
Sightings: P5 / ATOMIC-5 / LOOPS-4 (the CI entry point cannot be pointed at an
external packs directory - sighted three times, at every pack count); P6 / ATOMIC-6
(the layer graph cannot see the PrologAI packs the arm sits on); P7 / ATOMIC-7 (layer
numbers are a per-repo namespace with no global coordinate); N3 (the rule is declared
for 3 of 299 packs - adoption is real but partial, stated honestly in Wave 1.5).
Minimum remedy: a cross-repository layer scan that unions packs directories under a
shared coordinate, or a namespaced layer id, or an offset convention; a packs-dir
argument on the CI entry point; and a standing adoption program.

Underlying gap E-2 (intra-pack). The layer construct is PACK-GRANULAR - the pack is
the smallest unit it can layer, isolate for coupling, or test - so anything a coarse
pack puts INSIDE itself falls below the language's resolution.
Sightings: LOOPS-1 (a coarse pack collapses the internal layer hierarchy the finer
arms kept); LOOPS-2 (no intra-pack module boundary for COUPLING, so the zero-reference
guarantee needs a bespoke section-aware linter); LOOPS-3 (no intra-pack module
boundary for TESTABILITY, so an internal region cannot be exercised without the whole
loop). Minimum remedy: an intra-pack sub-module construct with an import/call boundary
the language enforces and a per-sub-module test target.

THEME F - COORDINATION AND CLOSURE PRIMITIVES (Lattice and actor ergonomics)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The closure hybrid (stigmergy plus notification) that Wave 1 delivered from L1/L2/L3
carried every reentrant loop in the program (Theme in Section Four). What remains are
bounded, additive ergonomics gaps on the substrate, several of them Medium:
 - L5 (spike, High, PARTIALLY CLOSED) and N4 (PrologAI, its general case, OPEN). A
   lower-layer actor can hold a higher-layer address as runtime DATA, unflagged. Wave
   1 delivered a heuristic lint for the literal-address case; the general case (a
   computed or dynamic address) needs a RUNTIME layer-aware transport, a different
   construct from the load-time checker. This is the one PARTIALLY CLOSED entry.
 - L6 (spike, Medium). publish/2 is fire-and-forget - no order, no backpressure, no
   durability - unsafe for a tight reentrant loop; the workaround uses the ordered
   receptor queue instead.
 - L7 (spike, Medium) and P9 (slice, Medium; second sighting). cyclic_actor has no
   bounded lifecycle, until-condition, or completion signal; every loop hand-rolls a
   done queue and external stop.
 - P8 (slice, Medium). lattice_await/5 matches on a relation FUNCTOR and returns if
   any matching fact exists, so it cannot await "a fact of relation R whose first
   argument is K"; a shared phase relation would busy-spin, so the slice gave each
   phase its own numbered relation as a workaround.
 - P10 (slice, Medium). Nothing binds "the static graph is acyclic" to "this runtime
   edge is the sanctioned closure of the loop" - the two proofs concern the same loop
   but are joined only in prose; a reentrant-loop descriptor construct would make them
   two checks on one declared object.
 - L8 (spike, Low). No built-in glass-box trace across actor hops; the spike supplies
   its own.
 - L9 (spike, Low). No in-memory nexus - opening one writes a growing /tmp journal.
 - N1 (PrologAI, unrated). lattice_transaction/2 is module-transparent but not
   declared a meta_predicate, an undocumented footgun.
 - N2 (PrologAI, unrated). thread_wait/2's modification wake does not fire on
   SWI-Prolog 9.0.4; code that assumed push reactivity is really polling.
 - N5 (PrologAI, unrated). No fairness or bound on the await waiter set.

THEME G - PACKAGING AND DEPENDENCY KINDS  (surfaced only by maximal granularity)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The underlying gap. PrologAI has ONE kind of dependency - a use_module import - and
no way to say what KIND it is. This bit only the one-pack-per-construct arm, whose
high pack count turned every intra-pack reference into an inter-pack import.
 - ATOMIC-1 (Medium). A STRUCTURE-only cross-pack reference (needed to mint a record,
   never to run behaviour) cannot be distinguished from a runtime dependency, so the
   layer graph and import fan-out are inflated by mint-time-only edges.
 - ATOMIC-2 (Medium). No dependency-AGGREGATION construct (a facade or bundle), so a
   consumer must enumerate every fine-grained pack it touches.
 - ATOMIC-3 (Medium). No cross-pack RECORD/ID registry, so content-addressed ids are
   threaded by hand-exported accessors and coupling concentrates at the interfaces.
 - ATOMIC-4 (Low). Co-locating a construct's structure with its runtime fuses the two
   dependency faces, so validating structure drags in the runtime substrate and
   running behaviour drags in the grounding engine; there is no way to load ONE face.
Minimum remedy: dependency annotations (structure-only versus runtime); a pack
group/facade; a cross-pack record registry; and a notion of a loadable pack "face."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME H - REFINEMENTS ON DELIVERED INVARIANTS, AND DESIGN OBSERVATIONS  (low)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Small open tails on constructs that are otherwise delivered, plus two decomposition
observations the Wave 3 verdict weighed:
 - N7 (PrologAI, binding freshness). The N6 binding reads a stratum's ordinal from the
   structure-record artifacts, with no check that they are current with the pack's
   minting code, so a stale artifact could pass a real drift. Remedy: a records-up-to-
   date gate, or a load-safe way to read an ordinal directly from its pack.
 - N13 (PrologAI, contract purity). The membership contract calls the test goal on
   every call and trusts it to be cheap and side-effect-free; nothing enforces it.
   Remedy: a purity/determinism lint or a double-negation-guarded call mode.
 - N15 (PrologAI, filtering mode). The once mode commits to the FIRST solution and
   refuses it if a non-member, even when a later solution is a member; there is no
   find-first-member (solution-filtering) mode. Deliberately not built (it risks a
   general solution-selection framework). Remedy: a distinct find_member mode if a
   future region needs it.
 - STRATA-1 (strata arm, Medium). The reentrant loop's three regions are CO-STRATAL
   (all brain regions, ordinal 9), so the runtime loop is single-stratum even though
   the structure it manipulates spans strata; no one code cut captures both. A design
   observation, remedy is recognition, not a construct.
 - STRATA-4 (strata arm, Low). One-pack-per-stratum produces wildly uneven packs (one
   empty cellular stratum against a fourteen-record region stratum). Explicitly NO
   language remedy - an inherent property of the rule, recorded so the verdict could
   weigh pack evenness.

SECTION THREE - THE CLOSED GAPS: THE TRACK RECORD

Every finding that a delivery closed, shown as finding-to-delivery, with the

PART ONE - THE SPIKE'S LATTICE AND LAYER WALLS, CLOSED IN WAVE 1

 - L1 (no lightweight non-semantic write door) -> DELIVERED: lattice_put/4,
   lattice_get/4, lattice_take/4, lattice_replace/4 on the backend-free base lattice
   module. Closing commit 17bdf77.
 - L2 (transaction journaling but not isolation) -> DELIVERED:
   lattice_transaction/3 with isolation(serializable), a per-nexus reentrant mutex;

 - L3 (no reactive await, so stigmergy busy-polls) -> DELIVERED: lattice_await/5

 - L4 (nothing expresses or enforces the strict layer rule) -> DELIVERED: the layer

PART TWO - THE ENFORCE-THEN-BUILD SPIRAL: A REGION'S WALL BECOMES THE NEXT REGION'S
FLOOR

   (the first real build on the enforced binding, 0 violations), the hippocampus, the
   cerebellum, and the amygdala - every stratum-primary region since.

 - ARBITER-1 (arbiter: PrologAI cannot express the membership invariant - a selected
   output is a member of the offered set, or an abstention - as a first-class checked
   property; it was hand-rolled three ways) -> DELIVERED as N8, the membership contract
   (a runtime postcondition via wrap_predicate/4: declare it, a non-member is refused
   with a glass-box error). Closing commit efe5049. This is the program's first RUNTIME
   invariant (L4 and N6 are load-time). RESTED ON BY: the hippocampus (recall - no
   confabulation, one declaration), the cerebellum (the committed correction - no
   illegal correction), and the amygdala (the committed appraisal - no undefined
   valence). What the arbiter proved by a 532-attempt hand-rolled battery, the later
   regions obtained by one line.

 - N9 / HIPPO-1 (the contract could read the offered set only from a plain LIST; the
   hippocampus's growing memory store is a set of facts on the Lattice, not a list, so
   it had to be flattened on every recall) -> DELIVERED as N11, the accessor form (a
   membership-TEST goal that never materialises the set - a single lookup, not an
   O(store size) copy; it can even guard an infinite set). Closing commit in the
   Wave 7 Part Two squash-merge (Work Package 427). RESTED ON BY: the cerebellum (its

SECTION FOUR - CROSS-CUTTING PATTERNS
================================================================================

The synthesis a scattered set of Ledgers cannot show.

PATTERN ONE - THE ONCE-MODE-DID-NOT-BITE PATTERN, AND WHERE NONDETERMINISM ACTUALLY
LIVES

The membership once mode (N14, and the per-solution scope note N10 before it) was
built for a predicate that GENERATES several candidate outputs on backtracking and
COMMITS one - the nondeterministic-select-then-commit shape. Three regions declared
it, and in all three it did NOT bite, because each commits a single DETERMINISTIC
output: the hippocampus recall (recorded at the time as "N10 did not bite"), the
cerebellum correction (CEREBELLUM-3), and the amygdala appraisal (AMYGDALA-3). The one
genuine nondeterministic-select-then-commit consumer in the whole program is the
ARBITER, the basal-ganglia selector - and it was built BEFORE the once mode existed,
so it hand-rolled its guard (which became N8). What this says about the architecture:
nondeterminism lives in SELECTION (the basal ganglia weighing candidate actions), not
in storage, correction, or appraisal, which each compute one answer. The once mode is
correct and waiting for its genuine consumer; on the deterministic regions it is a
true statement of intent that changes nothing. A construct that "did not bite" is a
finding about the shape of the architecture, recorded, not hidden.

PATTERN TWO - THE CROSS-STRATAL SEAM LINEAGE

The unmanaged cross-stratal seam is the program's most persistent gap. First recorded
as the slice's P1/P2 (cortisol's ten-stratum skip), it recurred as STRATA-2 (spanning
constructs have no home) and STRATA-5, as ARBITER-2 (the override's five-stratum skip,
adding the honest-ignorance distinction), and again as HIPPO-3, CEREBELLUM-2, and
AMYGDALA-2 (consolidation and cortisol skips). It returns wherever a process
legitimately spans non-adjacent strata - which, in a layered architecture, is often.
Causalontology's skips:true faithfully records THAT a cut jumped; what is missing is a
construct that manages the jump (see Theme B).

PATTERN THREE - WHAT THE ENFORCED INVARIANTS CARRIED (THE POSITIVE RESULT)

The program is as honest about what does NOT need building as about what does. Where a
region rested on an enforced invariant, it needed to invent nothing, and its Ledger
came out THIN:
 - N6 (the binding) aligned every stratum-primary region's pack layers with its
   stratum ordinals with zero strain and zero violations (arbiter, hippocampus,
   cerebellum, amygdala). The layer order fell OUT of the stratum ordinals for free,
   and the ten-stratum cortisol skip became the cleanest downward edge, not the
   hardest (the strata arm's headline result).
 - N8 (the membership contract) turned a behavioural safety property that the arbiter
   proved by a 532-attempt hand-rolled battery into a ONE-LINE declaration for the
   hippocampus (no confabulation), the cerebellum (no illegal correction), and the
   amygdala (no undefined valence).
 - N11 (the accessor form) let a growing store, a continuous range, and a
   context-dependent set each be guarded without ever materialising it.
 - The closure hybrid (L1/L2/L3) carried every reentrant loop - the slice's 3N+1
   loop, the hippocampus CA3 completion, the cerebellum correction, the amygdala
   conditioning - with zero actor-to-actor references, no busy-poll, and no hang,
   proven non-vacuously each time by an injection test.
The result: the region Ledgers are thin (hippocampus 3, arbiter 2, cerebellum and
amygdala 2 gaps plus 1 honesty each), and their remaining findings cluster on what is
genuinely NOT yet built (temporal, cross-stratal, affect, the grounding seam), not on
the safety, binding, and closure axes the platform now covers.

PATTERN FOUR - THE GROUNDING OF STRUCTURE IS SOLID; EVERY GAP IS DYNAMICS OR SEAM

Across the slice and all three granularity arms, the seventeen-kind Causalontology
2.0.0 schema layer and the Ed25519 signing/identity engine validated every minted
record with NO friction, and the twenty-eight slice records came out BYTE-IDENTICAL
under all three decompositions (proven by regenerating and comparing) - so the
content-addressed identity model is decomposition-agnostic. Not one finding in the
whole program is about the structure vocabulary itself. Every gap is about the
DYNAMICS (Theme C), the SEAM between structure and dynamics (Theme C) or between
strata (Theme B), TIME (Theme A), AFFECT (Theme D), or the coordination and packaging
ERGONOMICS around them. The grounding half of PrologAI is built; the dynamical and
temporal halves, and the seams that join them, are the work that remains.

PATTERN FIVE - THE LAYER RULE IS A SINGLE-REPOSITORY, PACK-GRANULAR CONSTRUCT

L4 was delivered as a single-repository, pack-granular checker, and every finding
about it since is a facet of those two boundaries: it cannot see ACROSS repositories
(P6, ATOMIC-6, P7, ATOMIC-7) and cannot see INSIDE a pack (LOOPS-1/2/3). The rule is
sound at its own resolution; the open work is extending its resolution outward (a
global coordinate) and inward (an intra-pack sub-module).

SECTION SIX - WHAT REMAINS TO MAKE PROLOGAI COMPLETE FOR THE CONNECTOME
================================================================================

An honest, prioritized forward list, drawn ONLY from the open gaps in Section Two -
the shortest list of PrologAI capabilities still missing before the language could
build an arbitrary brain region, interface, neurotransmitter, or hormone without a
workaround. Prioritized by how often a gap recurred and how load-bearing it is.
Nothing here is claimed beyond what the open gaps support.

THE FOUR LOAD-BEARING CAPABILITIES (the hard walls; build these first)

 1. A TEMPORAL AND SCHEDULING AFFORDANCE. A way to run a process after a delay, at a
    tick, or over time - a Lattice timer or a deferred-reactivation construct - AND an
    ordinal/tick temporal unit in the Causalontology temporal model so discrete-tick
    timing can be recorded natively. This is the only High-severity gap a region hit
    as a wall on its DEFINING function (the cerebellum's timing), and it recurred and
    sharpened. Every time-based process is blocked on it. Sources: HIPPO-2,
    CEREBELLUM-1.

 2. A MANAGED CROSS-STRATAL SEAM CONSTRUCT. A first-class way to record an intentional
    cross-stratal jump as more than a boolean: whether an intervening mechanism exists
    but is unmodeled versus does not exist (the honest-ignorance distinction); the
    option to draw the jump as a chain of adjacent-stratum steps; and a checkable HOME
    rule for a construct that belongs to an EDGE between strata. This is the program's
    MOST-RECURRING gap - six sightings across the slice, three regions, and the strata
    arm. Sources: P1, P2, STRATA-2, ARBITER-2, HIPPO-3, CEREBELLUM-2, AMYGDALA-2.

 3. A STRUCTURE-TO-DYNAMICS BINDING CONSTRUCT. A way to bind a grounded Causalontology
    record to the named native law or Lattice signal that realizes it, by one
    identity - a modulator/hormone construct, or a "realized_by" reference, or a typed
    signal relation - so the glass box can cross from what a construct is (structure)
    to how it behaves (dynamics). The grounding rule is correct; the seam it leaves is
    the point. Sources: P1, P3, P4, STRATA-5.

 4. A FIRST-CLASS AFFECTIVE-STATE CONSTRUCT. A persisted, modulatory valence/salience
    (or mood) that colours later processing, plus a context-aware membership accessor
    that can consult that held context when judging legality - so affect need not be
    smuggled into a value or carried as ad-hoc data. The whole limbic faculty rests on
    it. Source: AMYGDALA-1.

THE ERGONOMIC AND STRUCTURAL REFINEMENTS (real, but not hard walls)

 5. THE LAYER CONSTRUCT'S REACH. A cross-repository layer coordinate (a global or
    namespaced layer id, or an offset convention), a packs-directory argument on the
    CI entry point, and a standing adoption program; and, inward, an intra-pack
    sub-module boundary the language enforces for layering, coupling, and testability.
    Recurs many times, mostly Medium and Low. Sources: P5/P6/P7, ATOMIC-5/6/7,
    LOOPS-1/2/3/4, N3.

 6. COORDINATION-SUBSTRATE ERGONOMICS. A bounded cyclic_actor (an until-condition,
    max-cycles, and completion hook); an await that matches a bound argument PATTERN;
    a reentrant-loop descriptor binding static acyclicity to runtime closure; an
    ordered/durable publish; an in-memory nexus; a glass-box trace hook; a runtime
    layer-aware transport (the general case of L5); and the small platform notes N1,
    N2, N5. Sources: L5/N4, L6, L7/P9, L8, L9, P8, P10, N1, N2, N5.

 7. PACKAGING AND DEPENDENCY KINDS. A way to say what KIND a dependency is
    (structure-only versus runtime), a pack facade/bundle, a cross-pack record
    registry, and a loadable pack "face." These bit only the maximal-granularity arm;
    a role-based decomposition (the Wave 3 verdict's choice) largely avoids them.
    Sources: ATOMIC-1/2/3/4.

 8. INVARIANT AND BINDING REFINEMENTS. A records-freshness gate for the N6 binding
    (N7); a purity/determinism lint for the membership test goal (N13); and a
    find-first-member mode for the contract if a future region needs it (N15). Small
    tails on otherwise-delivered constructs. Sources: N7, N13, N15.

THE HONEST BOTTOM LINE

PrologAI's GROUNDING half - the seventeen-kind schema, content-addressed identity,
Ed25519 signing, the strict layer rule, the layer-to-stratum binding, and the runtime
membership contract with its accessor and once modes - is built and load-bearing, and
the enforced invariants carried four regions with thin Ledgers. What remains, to build
an arbitrary piece of Nature's Cognitive Architecture without a workaround, is
concentrated and nameable: TIME, the CROSS-STRATAL SEAM, the STRUCTURE-TO-DYNAMICS
SEAM, and AFFECT - four capabilities - with a tail of layer-reach, coordination, and
packaging ergonomics behind them. That is the forward agenda the program was built to
produce.
