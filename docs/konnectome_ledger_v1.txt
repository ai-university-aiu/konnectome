KONNECTOME LEDGER - THE RUNNING SCOREBOARD OF THE BUILD  (v1)

This is the single, canonical, navigable record of every wall the konnectome build
hits, exactly as PrologAI_Requirements_Ledger_v1.txt was for the language. Every wall
becomes an entry here FIRST; from here it is routed to exactly one destination: the
causalontology change order, the PrologAI change order, or a konnectome construct.
A wall shown to be UNNECESSARY is recorded as honestly as a wall that needed building:
honest non-closure is a real result, not a failure. Findings are DISCOVERED by
building, never guessed from the armchair (Second, Third, and Fourth Commandments).

Author: D. R. Dison / ai-university-aiu.

Acronyms are spelled out in full on first use, with the acronym in parentheses, and the
bare acronym thereafter. External proper names are kept verbatim (SWI-Prolog, Ed25519,
Git, GitHub, Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus). A causal_relation_object is a
Causalontology structure record, spelled in full per the Twelfth Commandment. konnectome
is a "Whole-Word System": no abbreviation series, no single-letter series - Loop-1 not
L1, Native-1 not N1, Proto-1 not P1.


================================================================================
SECTION ONE - WHAT THIS IS AND HOW TO READ IT
================================================================================

PART ONE - THE LEDGER'S ROLE

konnectome is a Proto-Artificial-General-Intelligence (Proto-AGI) prototype written in
PrologAI, storing its thoughts as Causalontology 3.0.0 records, and built from the
appendices of Nature's Cognitive Architecture. The build method is enforce-then-build,
inherited from the PrologAI program: a wall the build hits becomes an entry here; a
load-bearing entry is routed to exactly one place; the next slice rests on the result
rather than re-inventing it. This ledger is the product's honest scoreboard.

PART TWO - STATUS MEANINGS

 - OPEN: recorded, not yet resolved. The build hit a wall; no destination decided yet.
 - ROUTED: the finding passed its gate and was written to one destination - the
   causalontology change order (Second Commandment), the PrologAI change order (Third
   Commandment), or a named konnectome construct (Fourth Commandment).
 - CLOSED: the destination delivered - a Causalontology change-order entry, a PrologAI
   change-order entry, or a konnectome module - and the build now rests on it. Every
   CLOSED entry names what closed it.
 - UNNECESSARY: the wall was shown, by building, not to need a change at all. Recorded
   as honestly as a wall that needed building.

PART THREE - THE THREE ROUTING DESTINATIONS (route each finding to EXACTLY ONE)

 - DATA-STRUCTURE gap (must be SHARED across agents or repositories as a signed,
   evidence-graded, content-addressed record, not merely computed inside one konnectome
   runtime) -> docs/Causalontology_4_0_0_CHANGE_ORDER_v2.txt. Gated by the Thirteenth
   Commandment: must keep the 119-vector conformance suite green.
 - LANGUAGE gap (a real, load-bearing PrologAI-language wall) ->
   docs/PrologAI_CHANGE_ORDER_v1.txt. Additive only, gated by the Thirteenth
   Commandment: must not regress ARC-AGI-1 (400 of 400) or ARC-AGI-2 (120 of 120)
   through Mentova.
 - konnectome-SOLVABLE gap (expressible inside konnectome's own code) -> a named
   konnectome pack or module, recorded in the coverage list of Section Four.
